About Us

RMM High School was founded in 2006 to give underprivileged children an access to high quality education at a very low cost.
With only 2 classrooms, 4 teachers and 33 students back then to the flourishing 7-story infrastructure today, RMMHS houses a playgroup, Pre-primary, primary and secondary school with dedicated library, a science lab and computer lab within the premises. All classrooms are equipped with a television to facilitate e-learning and storytelling methods that make learning fun. Besides, education field trips, cultural programs, festive celebrations, sports day and annual days are celebrated for their all-round development.

Our students have won us laurels in the inter school competitions.

Today, over 500 students take advantage of the school facilities every year. Our students pass-out rate is 100% in the SSC Board. All of them have secured an admission in Mumbai’s renowned colleges. Some of the theme are pursuing their engineering and C.A courses. Our students have grown to be confident, successful and responsible adults today, and are successful professionals in the career of their choice. We are humbled to have been awarded as ‘A’ Grade School by the Department of Education in the year 2017-18. Today the RMMHS can make ends meet and show a small profit at the end of its financial year.

Online – in our endeavor to provide education to our school children, online classes have been conducted. Many children who did not have smart phones have been provided with new phones. Uptil now 15 phones have been distributed and we are still continuing.
